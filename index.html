About Me

My name is Karen Omelchuk, and I was born in Vernon, British Columbia. I now live in the vibrant community of Kelowna with my husband, Kevin Kerstens, and our two wonderful children, Harper and Paisley.

My passion for upcycling furniture started when I decided to repair a cherished piece inherited from my late mother, Janet Omelchuk. This project ignited a love for transforming old, discarded items into beautiful, functional pieces. It wasn't long before this hobby blossomed into my small business, Old Milk Truck Restorations. Operating out of my garage, Old Milk Truck Restorations is a one-woman operation where I meticulously restore and rejuvenate furniture, giving it a new lease on life.

I believe in the power of sustainability and the joy of creativity, which is reflected in every piece I work on. Each item has a unique story and character, and I take pride in preserving that while adding my personal touch.

Thank you for visiting Old Milk Truck Restorations and supporting my journey in furniture upcycling!
